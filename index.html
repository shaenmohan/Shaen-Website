NIKE AI SPEC COMMERCIAL

25 Second Film
(Final Version)

Creative Brief

Design and produce a cinematic AI-generated Nike spec commercial that captures resilience, speed, and determination through dramatic visual storytelling, premium sound design, and modern AI filmmaking techniques.

Role
◈ Creative Direction
◈ Prompt Engineering
◈ Editing

Tools Used
FCPX Krea.ai Kling.ai Elevenlabs.io Pixabay
↓
Behind the Scenes
Prompt Engineering

Iterated 40+ prompt variations across Krea and Kling to refine framing, lighting and athlete motion.

Motion Generation

Converted keyframes into AI-generated cinematic video using Kling AI.

Color Grading

Applied shot-specific cinematic grading instead of a single LUT to create a premium commercial look.

Editing

Assembled the commercial, voice-over, SFX and pacing inside Final Cut Pro.

Sound Design

Layered footsteps, cloth movement, whooshes, ambience, voice-over and music.

↓
Prompt Process
01

Concept & Ideation

Developed the creative vision — identifying the mood, pace, and visual language for the spec ad. Researched Nike's brand DNA to align the AI output with authentic brand storytelling.

02

Prompt Engineering

Crafted detailed, iterative prompts in Krea.ai and Kling.ai — refining cinematic framing, lighting direction, athlete motion, and product placement across multiple generation cycles.

03

Edit, Grade & Sound

Assembled the final cut in FCPX, applied cinematic color grading for a consistent tonal palette, and layered sound design using ElevenLabs and Pixabay to complete the narrative.
